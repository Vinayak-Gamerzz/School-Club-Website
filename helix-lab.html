Helix Lab

Advanced Robotics, Automation & Hardware Innovation

Helix Lab is the experimental core of Elyra where ideas turn into real machines. This is where members step beyond theory and start building actual working systems. From designing mechanical structures to programming smart controllers, Helix Lab focuses on hands-on learning. You don’t just learn how things work — you create them yourself. Whether it's automation, intelligent devices, or custom builds, this space is for those who want to engineer the future.

🚀 What You'll Work On

⚙️

Mechanical Builds

Create physical systems using gears, structures, and moving parts. Learn how real machines are designed and built from scratch.

🔋

Hardware Systems

Work with circuits, sensors, and electrical components. Build systems that interact with the real world.

🧠

Smart Controllers

Program microcontrollers like Arduino and ESP boards. Turn ideas into working automated systems.

👁️

Intelligent Machines

Build systems that can see, detect, and respond. Explore AI-powered robotics and automation.

🏁

Build Challenges

Take part in internal builds and competitions. Test your skills and improve through real challenges.

🧪

Innovation Zone

Experiment with new ideas and technologies. Work on unique projects beyond basics.
