Where Ideas Become Reality

Welcome to Elyra Club - the official Hack Club at Gagan Public School. We are a student-led community where we don't just learn technology, we build real projects.

From websites and apps to games and robotics, we turn creativity into reality. Powered by Hack Club, we get access to global resources, workshops and opportunities.

Whether you're a beginner or experienced, this is the place where you grow,build and innovate with like-minded creators.
