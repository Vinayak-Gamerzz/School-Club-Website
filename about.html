🚀 About Our Club

Elyra is a student-led innovation club where ideas turn into real projects.

We focus on learning by building rather than just studying theory. Members explore different fields through our skill arenas like Appocalypse, Nexus Forge, Helix Lab, and Aether Arena.

We encourage creativity, collaboration, and real-world problem solving. Elyra helps students develop skills and confidence for the future.

💡 How We Do It

We follow a hands-on approach where members learn by building real projects.

Students choose their area of interest and start working with guidance and resources. Collaboration and teamwork are encouraged to solve real problems.

We focus on consistency, practice, and improvement rather than just theory.

⚙️ How We Work

Members select projects based on their interests and skill level.

They work individually or in teams to build and complete them. Progress is tracked, and achievements are recognized through badges and milestones.

The goal is continuous learning, building, and leveling up skills over time. 🚀
